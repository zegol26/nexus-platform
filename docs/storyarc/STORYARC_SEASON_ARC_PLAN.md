# StoryArc Season Arc Plan

Three seasons, one per grade. 9 episodes per season = 3 arcs × 3 episodes (27 Story Mode items initial release). The 180-item target adds a second 9-episode wave per season by extending these arcs, without new structures. Episode IDs match `STORYARC_CONTENT_MANIFEST.md`.

Thematic ladder: Grade 10 adaptation/school life/friendship/communication/clubs → Grade 11 collaboration/conflict/responsibility/projects/presentations/decisions → Grade 12 future direction/education/vocational identity/internship/career/interviews/important choices. The "new student" premise occurs once, in S1E1.

## Structural rules

1. Every episode applies ≥2 same-grade School Core items; every episode from S1E2 onward contains ≥1 recall of earlier content.
2. Set-piece recall episodes: S1E8 (Campus Tour), S2E8 (Festival Day), S3E5 (The Mistake), S3E9 (Graduation).
3. Quests are communication goals; rewards are story progress, relationship change, and expression unlocks — never mastery.
4. Season finales resolve the season question and seed the next season's premise.
5. Both SMA and SMK futures are treated as first-class successful outcomes in Season 3; the interview episode ships university and job variants.
