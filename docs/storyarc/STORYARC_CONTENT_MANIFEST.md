Design manifest for the initial release. IDs are stable content identities; payloads are generated in Step 4 against the actual Codex schema/import contract. Allocation invariant: 90 = 45 School Core / 27 Story Mode / 18 Exam Lab; 30 per grade (15/9/6 each).

Legend — Skills: L listening, S speaking, R reading, W writing, G grammar, V vocabulary (primary listed first, supporting in parentheses). Evidence intent: EXP exposure, PRA practice, ASE assessed evidence, REC recall evidence. Track: SC School Core, SM Story Mode, EX Exam Lab. Phase: E (G10), F (G11–12). Full objective/recall linkage lives in `STORYARC_LEARNING_JOURNEY_MATRIX.md`; expression sets in `STORYARC_VOCABULARY_EXPRESSION_STRATEGY.md`.
